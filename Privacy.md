# Privacy Policy for SubZero

**Effective Date:** December 16, 2025

**SubZero: Subscription Tracker** ("the App") respects your privacy. We believe your financial data belongs to you and only you.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

*   **Local Storage:** All subscription information, costs, and preferences you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData/CoreData framework.
*   **iCloud Sync:** If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your data may sync across your own devices via your private iCloud account. We (the developers) do not have access to this data.

## 2. Third-Party Access

The App does **not** use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising SDKs. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## 3. Data Retention

Since we do not collect your data, we do not have a retention policy. Your data remains on your device until you delete the App or delete the data within the App.

## 4. Your Consent

By using SubZero, you consent to this privacy policy.
