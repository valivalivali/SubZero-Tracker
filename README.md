### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. SubZero does not connect to your bank account and does not upload your data to any external servers. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud if enabled.

**Q: How do I delete a subscription?**
A: Swipe left on any subscription in the list to delete it, or tap on it to open the edit screen and scroll down to "Delete".

**Q: Can I request a new service preset?**
A: Absolutely! Please open an issue on this GitHub repository or contact us via email.
